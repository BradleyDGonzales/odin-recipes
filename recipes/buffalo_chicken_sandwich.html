Buffalo Chicken Sandwich contents

Description

This recipe gives you exactly all the information you need to track what you just ate, nothing better than a buffalo chicken sandwich to fill in your cravings.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tsp. chili powder
  • 1 tsp. paprika
  • Cooking spray
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1/4 cup buffalo sauce
  • 4 tsp. butter
  • 1/4 cup nonfat plain Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup blue cheese
  • 4 reduced calorie hamburger buns

Steps

  1. Slice or pound the chicken into thinner cutlets. Spray with cooking spray and season with chili powder, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  2. Heat a grill, grill pan, or skillet over medium high heat. Spray with cooking spray if needed. Cook the chicken for 3-4 minutes per side or until cooked through.
  3. Meanwhile mix together the yogurt and blue cheese to create a sauce for the burgers. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Melt the butter and stir it into the buffalo sauce. Toss the cooked chicken breasts in this mixture.
  5. Assemble the sandwiches and add lettuce, tomatoes, onions, and any other toppings you like.
